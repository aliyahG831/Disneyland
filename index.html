The Rides

For the sake of space I will only be talking about rides from Magic Kingdom. The rides in Magic Kingdom are classics but also most of them are kind of boring. Don't get me wrong not all of them are boring but I would say the ones in Fantasyland are. EXCEPT the tea cup ride, that is the only exception. I would say the worst ride in Fantasyland, actually the worst ride in the park, is the Matterhorn Bobsleds. That ride is straight up traumatizing. Everything about that ride is terrible, the painful seats, you get wet like c'mon, and the scary a** yeti animatronic. I will never get on that ride ever again.
